Self Care in a Glass

Self care is difficult for everyone. This game is meant more to help you reflect on how much or how little self-care you're doing, or if you're allowing yourself to do too much. It's also meant to help think about what might be harder or easier for you. The way I weighted the tasks might not match to everyone.

  • Your goal is to fill the glass.
  • You can reset the glass at any time.
  • Click on the tasks as you complete them.
