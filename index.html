Welcome to this self care game

Self care is difficult for everyone. This game is meant more to help you reflect on how much or how little self-care you're doing, or if you're allowing yourself to do too much.

  • Your goal is to fill up the glass to 100% without overfilling
  • You can reset the glass at any time
  • Click on the tasks as you complete them
  • Some of the tasks can only be completed a limited number of times, so be careful!

KEEP GOING!
